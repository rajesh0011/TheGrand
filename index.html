An Immersive Experience in The Heart of New Delhi

The Grand New Delhi, nestled in the heart of Delhi NCR, is a distinguished luxury hotel. The ideal destination for the discerning business and leisure traveller, our hotel boasts modern facilities in a resort-like setting with luscious greens & waterscape.

The Grand New Delhi is situated 7 km from IGI airport and in close proximity to Aerocity, Embassies and Consulates based in Vasant Vihar. We are a short drive away from major business hubs of Delhi, Emabssies and Counsulates in at Chanakyapuri as well as UNESCO World Heritage site Qutub Minar. Our 381 elegantly-designed rooms and suites, 4 dining destinations and much-acclaimed Oasis Spa make us one of the most popular 5-star luxury hotels in New Delhi. Featuring an iconic swimming pool, a fitness centre, and state-of-the-art event venues spread over 30,000 sq.ft.
