Facilities

The experienced staff at The Grand New Delhi caters to the needs of our guests by providing an extensive range of services and facilities. Should you require a service not listed here, please contact us and we will do our best to assist you. Book your stay with our hotel near Delhi airport.

Swimming Pool

Dive into crystal-clear waters that reach a comfortable 5 feet. Whether you’re doing laps or simply floating, you’ll find the depth just right for your aquatic adventures. A generous 25 meters in both length and width, our adult pool provides ample space for you to stretch out and enjoy your swim. Our littlest guests have their own dedicated pool, measuring 1.5 feet in depth. It’s the perfect spot for splashing, giggles, and water play. The pool is open from 7 Am to 8 Pm daily. Whether you’re an early riser or prefer an evening dip, we’ve got you covered.

Fitness Center

Whether you’re an early riser, a night owl, or somewhere in between, our Fitness Center is ready whenever you are. With 24/7 access, you can squeeze in a workout at your convenience. Our dedicated gym trainers are here to support you on your fitness journey. From personalized workout plans to form corrections, they’ll ensure you get the most out of every session. You’ll find our trainers ready to assist you from 6:30 am to 11:00 pm. Need a spotter or advice on that deadlift? They’ve got you covered.
